Whispering Flow - Whanganui River

Oil on canvas

In my quest to capture picturesque moments for my paintings, I often find myself drawn to serene locations by the water. One such occasion led me to the meeting point of the Taringamotu River and the Wanganui River. Nestled in this tranquil setting, I was greeted by the gentle caress of sunlight, casting its golden rays upon the scene. As I sat by the water's edge, my eyes were captivated by the remarkable sight of rocks gracefully emerging from the surface, creating a striking contrast against the submerged stones. Across the river, a line of majestic trees stood proudly, their branches reaching towards the heavens. The sky above adorned itself with a delightful display of fluffy clouds, lending a touch of whimsy to the vista. In this painting, I aim to transport you to that very moment, allowing you to feel the warmth of the sun, witness the juxtaposition of rocks, and appreciate the harmonious dance between the verdant trees and the ever-changing sky.
