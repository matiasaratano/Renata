About

A bit about myself...

Hi there!

My name is Renata Nemeth, and I am an award-winning visual artist who lives and works in New Zealand.

My love for art and creativity started when I was young and has only grown stronger over the years.

Throughout my studies, I have become particularly passionate about painting and mixing colours.

What really inspires me is the natural world around us. I find so much beauty and serenity in nature, and I love exploring that in my artwork.

My goal as an artist is to create a sense of calm and peace through my pieces. I want to help people connect with the world around them and find beauty in even the smallest things.
